About Us

Beyond Just Work is a Gurugram based coworking and virtual space venture that was founded in 2019 with the vision of being a home to startups and helping them build their business in the most optimal and efficient way.

Our Vision

Beyond Just Works: Elevating activities for personal growth, work-life balance, networking, creativity, and broader horizons, fostering a vibrant social exchange.

Our Experience

Through the years, we've consistently delivered paramount security, comfort, and top-tier services. Our track record boasts high-quality cleaning, ample parking, wellness amenities, high-speed internet, and inviting outdoor spaces - all aimed at delighting our cherished clients.
